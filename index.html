Connecting Blue-Collar Workforce to Global Opportunites

Workezy bridges the gap between surplus blue-collar workforce in countries like India and labor shortages in regions such as the GCC and the West.

About Us

The demand for blue-collar workforce is rising in regions like the GCC and the West, while countries like India have a surplus of skilled workers. Workezy leverages this opportunity to connect talent with global opportunities. With 3 years of experience in the recruitment industry, Workezy offers end-to-end services, including workforce sourcing, upskilling, and visa and immigration assistance. We help employers overcome labor shortages and provide workers with stable, well-paying jobs backed by a job guarantee

Industries We Serve

Our Process

1

UNDERSTANDING EMPLOYEE REQUIREMENTS

We work closely with employers to understand their specific manpower needs, including skills, experience, and timelines.

2

SCREENING AND SHORTLISTING CANDIDATES

Two rounds of interviews are conducted to screen and shortlist candidates based on employer standards and job roles

3

ASSESSMENT AND DOCUMENTATION

We conduct trade skills and language assessments, organize interviews with employers, and verify documents with support for any corrections

4

VISA AND IMMIGRATION

We manage the visa process, assist with documentation, and prepare candidates for interviews to ensure a smooth transition.
